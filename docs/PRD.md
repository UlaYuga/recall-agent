See [PM Delivery Artifacts](PM_DELIVERY_ARTIFACTS.md) for the full source pack.

## Product

Recall - AI CRM Reactivation Pipeline.

## Problem

Operators lose a large share of registered users after first activity. Dormant users represent a lost LTV opportunity, while generic mass outreach produces weak reactivation.

## Target User

CRM and retention managers who need scalable personalized campaigns with approval control and measurable ROI.

## MVP Scope

- Mock event bus.
- Dormant trigger and rule-based cohort classifier.
- LLM script and prompt generation.
- Approval dashboard.
- Runway video pipeline.
- Telegram delivery PoC.
- Landing and tracking.
- Metrics and ROI simulation.
